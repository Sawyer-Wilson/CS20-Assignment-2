Who we are

The Tufts of Grass Landscaping Company was started by three friends who grew up helping their families and neighbors with odd landscaping projects. Eventually, we came together realizing that we had the experience and tools necessary to found our own company. We have now been providing high quality landscape services to homeowners throughout the northeast for three years. Our success is rooted in our devotion and loyalty to our customers.

  • Respecting the Earth
